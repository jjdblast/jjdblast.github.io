HiWeGo

We present HiWeGO, an app which can be used to watch AR soccer match on your mobile device. Please have a look at this Introduction video to enjoy.

To convert soccer match video into AR view, monocular depth estimation and 3d reconstruction tech are used, the pipeline of our work is presented below:

First, we implemented tracking by detection algorithms in monocular soccer match video, then we can get each player’s mask by mask-rcnn, and their pose by pose estimation (in this work, we use OpenPose).

The mask and pose are needed by our depth estimation model, we use a mulit-head CNN to predict each player’s depth.
