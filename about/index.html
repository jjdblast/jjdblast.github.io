About Me

I’m Jiandong Jin, a master student major in computer science in Peking University (expected to graduate in 2020). I received my Bachelor degree in Transportation Engineering from Tongji University. Now I’m working at Computer Center of Peking University, and also interning at Tongji-MIT City Science Lab @ Shanghai since this summer.

My current research interest focuses on urban computing and intelligent transportation systems, developing innovative methodologies and applications to address efficiency and sustainability issues in transportation systems. I am particularly interested in integrating advances in machine learning into urban perception, agent-based simulation, and traffic flow modeling.
